⚠ Security Warning

Don't share your password with anyone — ever.

Google will never ask for your password via email, phone, or any third-party site.

Never share your credentials with colleagues, IT staff, friends, or family.

Use a unique, strong password and enable 2-Step Verification for extra protection.
